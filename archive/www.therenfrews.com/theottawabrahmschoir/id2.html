Director

The Ottawa Brahms Choir Director

Christopher Askwith

Christopher Askwith graduated from Carleton University in 2006, having studied classical voice with Susan Blyth-Schofield and taken classes in composition (and, subsequently, conducting). He has been active in Ottawa’s choral scene for nearly twenty years, as a member of the Ottawa Regional Youth Choir, Carleton University Choir, Ottawa Choral Society, and Ottawa Bach Choir.


Christopher is a founding member of the Harmonia Choir of Ottawa, and remains with that choir, not only as a chorister, but also in the capacity of Composer-in-Residence. He has conducted choral workshops for youth and briefly conducted a small local choir, the Vista Singers, and looks forward to the opportunity and challenge of working with the Ottawa Brahms Choir.


Christopher lives in Ottawa with his wife and young son.
